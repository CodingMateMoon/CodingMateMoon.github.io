### ORACLE SID 환경변수 등록 - export ORACLE_SID=YOUR_DB_SID 
```console
bash-4.2# echo "export ORACLE_SID=ORCLCDB" >> ~/.bash_profile
bash-4.2# . ~/.bash_profile
bash-4.2# echo $ORACLE_SID
ORCLCDB
```
